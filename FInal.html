What role do these metrics play in bettering your business?

By analyzing sales data through key metrics like MAE, RMSE, and MSE, coupled with visual trends, businesses can gain a deeper understanding of their performance, accurately forecast future sales, identify patterns or anomalies, and make well-informed decisions to optimize operations, minimize risks, improve resource allocation, and capitalize on growth opportunities effectively.

What do these plots and graphs explain?

The plot compares Actual Sales (with markers) and Predicted Sales (with a dashed line). A close match between the two lines indicates the model is accurately forecasting sales, while significant divergence suggests the model's predictions need improvement. This visualization helps businesses evaluate the accuracy of their sales predictions.
